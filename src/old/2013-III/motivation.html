Motivation icons for Photoshop & Illustrator

When I saw the Logic Pro X icon I thought that the apps for creative work should have similar ones, because it's a beautiful example of an icon that motivates you. Without thinking twice I made two icons for Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator as these are the apps which I use in my everyday work.



"Mona Lisa" by Leonardo Da Vinci stands for raster graphics and the Photoshop and "Hope" by Shepard Perry — for vector graphics and the Illustrator.

Download icons (1.9 Mb)

Usage

You could use one of two ways from finder app or from terminal.

Copy files from Finder



  • Download icons and unzip
  • For Adobe Photoshop CS6 copy PS_AppIcon.icns to /Applications/Adobe Photoshop CS6/Adobe Photoshop CS6.app/Contents/Resources/
  • For Adobe Illustrator CS6 copy AI_Application_Icon.icns to /Applications/Adobe Illustrator CS6/Adobe Illustrator.app/Contents/Resources/

Copy files from Terminal



  • Clone git repo:
    git clone https://github.com/keske/motivation.git
  • Copy AI icon:
    cp motivation/icons/AI_Application_Icon.icns /Applications/Adobe\ Illustrator\ CS6/Adobe\ Illustrator.app/Contents/Resources
  • Copy PS icon:
    cp motivation/icons/PS_AppIcon.icns /Applications/Adobe\ Photoshop\ CS6/Adobe\ Photoshop\ CS6.app/Contents/Resources
